About Me

Ramita has been designing websites for quite a few years on her own. When she was younger, she would frequently build play websites for fun on hosting sites like Freewebs and Piczo. Later on as she was pursuing music, she was prompted to make a website that served as a portfolio and decided to do it herself.

Several years later, she re-kindled her interest for building websites and signed up for Codeacademy, learning HTML and CSS for the first time. She enjoyed it enough to sign up for the Full Stack Web Development major at Pasadena City College. She is continuing her journey from web design to development and has a great interest in Front End Engineering.
